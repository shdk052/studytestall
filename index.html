Part 1: Reading Comprehension (30 pts)

David is a student who loves literature. Last week, he spoke to his friend about a secret book. He assumed his friend would like it, but his friend refused to read it because he was busy. Despite this, David convinced him that the book provides a great experience. David offered to give him the book for free. Somehow, his friend changed his mind and read it. He felt positive about the story.

1. What does David love? (10 pts)

2. Why did the friend say NO at first? (10 pts)

3. How did the story make the friend feel? (10 pts)
