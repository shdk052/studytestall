A Special Invention

Last year, Maya had a great experience. She wanted to design a special mask for her grandmother. Maya assumed it would be difficult to invent something new, but she had plenty of ideas. Despite the fact that she was young, she spoke to her teacher and convinced him to help her. Her teacher offered her a clear plan. Maya made the mask and it was essential for her grandmother's health. Maya felt positive and happy.

David is a student who loves literature. Last week, he spoke to his friend about a secret book. He assumed his friend would like it, but his friend refused to read it because he was busy. Despite this, David convinced him that the book provides a great experience. David offered to give him the book for free. Somehow, his friend changed his mind and read it. He felt positive about the story and thanked David.

1. What subject does David love most? (10 pts)

2. Why did the friend say NO to the book at first? (10 pts)

3. How did the story make the friend feel? (10 pts)
